Pets Play Games

1️⃣ Fetch – Throw a ball or stick and let the dog bring it back.
2️⃣ Tug-of-War – Use a rope toy; dogs love to pull and play.
3️⃣ Hide and Seek – Hide somewhere and call your dog to find you.
4️⃣ Find the Treat – Hide treats around the house and let your dog sniff them out.
5️⃣ Obstacle Course – Make a small course using pillows, chairs, or cones.
6️⃣ Water Play – Splashing or fetching toys in water (safe area only).
